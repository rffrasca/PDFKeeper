Executing the PDF Upload as a Blocking Operation

In a multi-user environment, it may be necessary to execute the PDF upload as a blocking operation, preventing the user from performing other database transactions while the upload is executing.

  • To set the PDF Upload to execute as a blocking operation, apply SetBlockingUploadPolicy.reg to the targeted computers.

  • To clear the policy, apply ClearBlockingUploadPolicy.reg to the targeted computers.

Additional Information:

  • SetBlockingUploadPolicy.reg and ClearBlockingUploadPolicy.reg can be found in %LOCALAPPDATA%\Programs\PDFKeeper.

  • Elevated privileges are required to set or clear this policy.

  • This policy is ignored when using the single-user database option.
