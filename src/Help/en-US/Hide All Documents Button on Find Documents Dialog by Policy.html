Hide All Documents Button on Find Documents Dialog by Policy

In a multi-user environment with a database that contains thousands of documents, it may be necessary to limit who can find all documents.

  • To hide the All Documents button on the Find Documents dialog, apply SetHideAllDocumentsPolicy.reg to the targeted computers.

  • To clear the policy, apply ClearHideAllDocumentsPolicy.reg to the targeted computers.

Additional Information:

  • SetHideAllDocumentsPolicy.reg and ClearHideAllDocumentsPolicy.reg can be found in %LOCALAPPDATA%\Programs\PDFKeeper.

  • Elevated privileges are required to set or clear this policy.

  • PDFKeeper will need to be restarted for the change to take effect.

  • This policy is ignored when using the single-user database option.
