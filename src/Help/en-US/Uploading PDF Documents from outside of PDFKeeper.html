Uploading PDF Documents from outside of PDFKeeper

PDF documents can be uploaded from outside of PDFKeeper using the following methods:

  • From Web Browser/application, save the PDF document into PDFKeeper Upload, located in the Windows Documents and Downloads folders.

  • Using Windows Explorer, copy or move the PDF document(s) that you wish to upload and paste into PDFKeeper Upload, located in the Windows Documents and Downloads folders.

Additional Information:

  • PDFKeeper must be open for the PDFKeeper Upload folder shortcut to be available in the Windows Documents and Downloads folders.

  • PDF document(s) must meet the criteria specified in the Uploading PDF Documents topic; otherwise, any that don't will be rejected by the Upload process and remain in the Upload folder.

  • To automate the uploading of PDF documents, see Automating with the PDFKeeper Upload Process.
